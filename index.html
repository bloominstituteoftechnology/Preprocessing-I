Who Am I

I decided to enroll in Lambda School's Full Stack Web Development program in October of 2019. I have begun a beautiful journey, to expand my knowledge into an entirely different discipline. I have always been pretty good with computers and like to think I have a creative eye so i felt that this may be the perfect way to make a transition into a new career. I welcome the challenge and look forward to moving down a path less traveled.

Twelve years ago I fell in love with cooking. I strived to learn all that I could so that I could be the best I could be at my culinary craft. Only those who have worked in the industry themselves can begin to tell you the toll twelve years takes on a body. Constant standing, bending, lifting: long hours and very little time with those you love. Don't get me wrong, the memories I have made along the way and the knowledge I have gained is invaluable, but it was time for a change. I have a future to look forward to and I would like to enjoy it as much as possible. More time with friends, and the idea of branching out with a family of my own have taken precendence in life.
