What I'm About

I'm an arrogant and proud swordsman affiliated with AVALANCHE, and also a former member of an elite warrior unit called SOLDIER. I am now a mercenary, and uninterested in anything beyond my hired task at hand. I am always looking to discover more about my past, and with the help of my friends, I learn that there is more to being a hero than possessing physical strength and fame. I have developed compassion for the Planet and people I fight to protect. I fight to protect the Planet against Sephiroth.

I have extensive coding experience programming Shinra submarines and airships that use a custom gps system I have built to navigate Gaia. I am building a materia database that will store extensive information on the elements that I have gathered and used over the years.
